Key Terminology

Hydro Jetting
A high-pressure water technique used to clear clogs in pipes without causing damage.
Slab Leak Detection
Methods used to detect water leaks under a concrete foundation, often involving specialized equipment like moisture meters and acoustic sensors.
Sewer Camera Inspection
A diagnostic tool that uses a camera to visually inspect the inside of pipes for blockages or damage.
Trenchless Sewer Replacement
A method of replacing damaged sewer pipes without digging up the entire yard, minimizing disruption.

Common Questions

What is hydro jetting and when is it used?

Hydro jetting is a method used to clear blockages in pipes by forcing high-pressure water through the pipes. It’s ideal for clearing stubborn clogs and buildup.

How can I detect a slab leak in my home?

Slab leaks are typically detected using advanced equipment like moisture meters or acoustic sensors to locate hidden leaks beneath your home’s foundation.

What is trenchless sewer replacement?

Trenchless sewer replacement is a method used to replace damaged sewer pipes without digging up the yard, making it a less invasive option.
